# Employee FAQ

## What is Microsoft 365 Copilot?

Microsoft 365 Copilot is an AI assistant that helps users work inside Microsoft 365 tools such as Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams, and PowerPoint.

## Can Copilot see all company data?

No. Copilot works based on Microsoft 365 permissions. It can only access content the user already has permission to access.

## Can I trust everything Copilot produces?

No. AI output should always be reviewed for accuracy, tone, completeness, and policy compliance.

## Can I use Copilot with sensitive information?

Only if the use is approved and follows company policy. Employees should avoid entering confidential, personal, legal, financial, or protected information unless clearly authorized.

## What should I do if Copilot gives a bad answer?

Try improving the prompt, adding more context, or asking Copilot to explain its reasoning. If the issue continues, contact IT support.

## Who do I contact for help?

Contact the IT support team for access issues, policy questions, troubleshooting, or training support.
