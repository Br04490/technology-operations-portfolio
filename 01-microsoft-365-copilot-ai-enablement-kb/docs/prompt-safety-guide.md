# Prompt Safety Guide

## Purpose

This guide helps employees write safer and more effective prompts when using Microsoft 365 Copilot and approved AI tools.

## Safe Prompting Principles

Use prompts that are:

- Clear
- Specific
- Work-related
- Policy-compliant
- Free of unnecessary sensitive information

## Avoid Including

Do not include:

- Passwords
- Social Security numbers
- Personal health information
- Confidential client records
- Payment information
- Private HR details
- Legal strategy
- Unapproved proprietary data

## Strong Prompt Formula

Use this structure:

Task + Context + Format + Audience + Constraints

Example:

Summarize this Teams meeting for department managers. Include decisions, action items, owners, deadlines, and unresolved risks. Keep the tone professional and concise.

## Review Checklist

Before using AI output, ask:

- Is this accurate?
- Is anything missing?
- Is the tone appropriate?
- Does this include sensitive information?
- Does this follow company policy?
